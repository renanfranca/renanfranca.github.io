About me

Curious about me? Here you have it:

I've been in the realm of software engineering since 2008, making my way through various roles and responsibilities. With expertise in full stack development using Vue.js & Spring Boot, I've recently honed my skills to a more specialized domain. While I'm well-versed in handling both frontend and backend, I've realized that dedicating my focus singularly to either frontend or backend enhances my productivity.

At present, I've immersed myself in backend development, working part-time with Java and Spring Boot. I employ a plethora of modern tools and technologies like Docker, OpenShift, Redis, and Kubernetes to craft microservices that stand the test of time.

That's a snapshot of my journey so far. Here's to many more lines of code, family vacations, and movies in the days to come!
