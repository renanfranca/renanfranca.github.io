About me

Curious about me? Here you have it:

I've been in the realm of software engineering since 2008, making my way through various roles and responsibilities. With expertise in full stack development using Vue.js & Spring Boot, I've recently honed my skills to a more specialized domain. While I'm well-versed in handling both frontend and backend, I've realized that dedicating my focus singularly to either frontend or backend enhances my productivity.

At present, I've immersed myself in backend development, working part-time with Java and Spring Boot. I employ a plethora of modern tools and technologies like Docker, OpenShift, Redis, and Kubernetes to craft microservices that stand the test of time.

Previously, I was deeply immersed in frontend development. Converting intricate designs into dynamic HTML + CSS, coupled with JavaScript's task of interfacing with backend APIs, was a formidable challenge. As I grappled with the nuances of frameworks like AngularJS, React, and Vue.js, I identified a niche I deeply resonated with: translating Figma layouts into sleek HTML + CSS designs using TailwindCSS.

My academic journey commenced with a Bachelor's in Computer Science from 2005 to 2009. Elevating my knowledge base, I pursued a Postgraduate Degree in Information Security between 2018 and 2020, arming myself with the expertise to navigate the constantly evolving digital landscape.

Beyond the lines of code and intricate algorithms, I'm a fervent movie enthusiast. There's nothing I relish more than watching cinematic masterpieces. Moreover, traveling with my family serves as the perfect palette cleanser, giving me a fresh perspective and rejuvenating my spirits.

That's a snapshot of my journey so far. Here's to many more lines of code, family vacations, and movies in the days to come!
